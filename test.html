Email Entry Tests

If the entry is valid, a new entry is supposed to be appear as the last row of the log table.

To see the logs, just click on the extension button.

Test the logs when

  • 1. you enter your email you specified in the extension settings,
  • 2. you submit your email you specified in the extension settings.

IMPORTANT: There is no real submit in the background.

Test the logs when you just enter an email other than you specified in the extension settings.

Test the logs when you just enter email into a generic input box with or without submit.
